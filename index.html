Who We Are

The David Douglas Soccer Club is a non-profit, community based program dedicated to sharing our vision and love of the game of soccer. Located in SE Portland Oregon, DDSC Recreational Soccer League is a league available for children U5 through U14 who want to learn skills and play soccer purely for the fun of it.
